Cybersecurity Professionals



Summary

1. Life of a cybersecurity professional can vary in many aspects. In a youtube video called Cyber Security Day In the Life! (18 year old Cyber Security Engineer), an 18 year old cybersecurity engineer shared his daily life, we can see he showed us all of his activities in a day including his meals, working tasks, and talked about the task’s details. According to the video, it seems like his objective is to work on setting up some sort of a scanner that can scan devices on the company’s network for vulnerabilities which is called openvas. The video shows the time lapse of his activities, which were about doing research on the Internet, looking for reading materials and studying them. It is quite impressive that he was just 18 years old. Here is the link:

  • Cyber Security Day In the Life! (18 year old Cyber Security Engineer)


    • 2. A website called Varonis supports readers with information about a day in a life in cybersecurity. In the website, they have collected details of a day in the life of many job roles regarding cybersecurity in order to give us the best perspective about the domain. These details are backgrounds, day-to-day schedule, pros and cons of some job roles such as cybersecurity engineer, analyst, and other professionals. The website has revealed the insights of cybersecurity in so many aspects. Link to the website:

  • What Working in Cybersecurity is Really Like: A Day in the Life



    • Discussion

    The work of cybersecurity professionals can be diverse with a lot of tasks such as working with tools to look for vulnerabilities and improve the IT infrastructure, learning new technologies, working with clients and customers. As mentioned, they have to interact with customers to help them solve the problems, and collaborate with their co-workers or their team to do projects. Professionals in cybersecurity spend most of their time at their workplace doing research on the Internet. For example, an 18 year old cyber security engineer has to stay on his workstation to work with his company’s project for an entire day, or in most of the cybersecurity jobs, they have to be at their workplace for many hours ( about 10 hours according to Varonis) just to have time to work with their customers. According to these two web sources, the most challenging aspect is that there is no typical day in cybersecurity, everyday is a different problem and challenge that they have to tackle; therefore, there are always new things to learn and adapt.

The schedule starting at 8.45am and ending at 6.30pm onwards illustrated his daily life of being at work. After that, is his review of a day, in which he considered all of the things he committed, confirmed his best challenge on that day, and his motivation. Link to the website:

    • Discussion

    Being a web developer, there are some common kinds of work that any developer does such as, conducting meetings with clients or colleagues, website programming, testing and reviewing the applications, practicing pair programming or other teamwork activities. Just like other IT professionals, their work always requires meeting with clients, and other web developing colleagues at the workplace. It is likely that most of the time of a web developer is spent on working with websites at their workplace, on their workstation. According to Sam, a web developer in my second summary, his most challenging part of his productivity on a daily basis is receiving ad-hoc requests from other teams. Generally, the most challenging part for most web developers is to learn a new framework, language, etc. Although it is very interesting and amazing when mastering them, they can be very time-consuming.
